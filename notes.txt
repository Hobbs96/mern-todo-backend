running "killall -s KILL node" will terminate any node processes currently running, like nodemon

starting and stopping mongodb on linux is:
    "sudo service mongod start"
    "sudo service mongod stop"
    "sudo service mongod restart"
and opening a mongo terminal is simply "mongo" in the terminal
